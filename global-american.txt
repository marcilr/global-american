Fanless PC
==========
Compact, Reliable Computing Power.

Industrial strength, fanless low power systems are in high demand.
Global American is proud to offer a variety of fanless PCs that are
customizable to suit your needs.  These small form factor systems
feature a variety of motherboards, CPUs, memory and peripherals to
make sure you get the right system for the right project.

A Fanless PC is the ideal choice for applications that must be
reliable and quiet, and are used in rugged environments.  Any
application can benefit from a powerful system with no moving parts,
and you get that with Global American's line of products.  We
understand Fanless.
